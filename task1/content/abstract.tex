\begin{abstract}
This report gives some personal comments on selected parts of the book
``The Face of Human Rights'' by Walter Käin, Lars Müller and Jidith
Wyttenbach.

The comments intend to reflect the authors thaughts about the content
of some selected paragraphs and passages of the book. Either as a
general question about the topic discussed and mentioned facts or as an
expression of agreement or disagreement on the statements made by the
authors of the book.
\end{abstract}
